Look up! That's a static navigation bar. You can include your brand, links, dropdowns, inputs, etc. Navbars can be on either the top or bottom of the page and can be static, fixed, or sticky. Fixed means it will scroll with the page, static means it will not scroll with the page, and sticky means it will scroll with the page until it reaches the top or bottom where it will remain.
